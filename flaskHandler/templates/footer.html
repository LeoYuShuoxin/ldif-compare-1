About

LDIF Compare helps identify differences between LDIF files from typically two nodes.
Two type of outputs are supported:

  • 1. CSV table downloaded.
  • 2. Webpage table presented here.
Note: in CSV file, comma "," was replaced as "|" for cell segmentation, you could save it as a EXCEL table and replace "|" back to ",".
